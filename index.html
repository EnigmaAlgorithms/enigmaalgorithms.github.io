Royalties and Licensing

Enigma provides the embedded control software for our Hardware partners, under a licensing and royalty arrangement. Enigma does not control or produce hardware systems. Rather, we provide software control systems for hardware platforms developed by partners who meet our minimum technical requirements to support our embedded systems. "powered by Enigma"
